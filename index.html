Kiana Moore

I am a sophomore Honors Computer Information Systems major at Howard University from Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.

I aspire to be a software engineer. I have a passion for problem-solving and programming, and I am fascinated by technology and its capabilities. With the skills I develop during my career in software, I will become an entrepreneur and introduce my lifestyle app to the black community to help build confidence in the versatility of black hair. Once established, I hope to participate in additional community services such as mentorship and education. I eventually want to found a mentorship program for black girls without female role models and a voter education initiative in communities with less access to political education, starting with my hometown of Philadelphia. After building enough wealth to reinvest into my community, I plan to initiate a second career in real estate and learn how to successfully invest in providing more affordable, safe, and comfortable housing to those facing poverty or gentrification within their communities.
